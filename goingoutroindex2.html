Our Story

Kiley had the idea for Outro after two of her friends were drugged in the same month. After talking to more of her friends and hearing their stories, she decided something had to be done. Going out had gotten scary. She was frustrated and confused that there wasn’t a platform for girls, LBGTQ+ community members or people of color to find spaces where they can feel safe AND have fun. So she, along with Sarah, Anna, and Josh, set out to find a solution.

2 out of 3 women have been verbally or physically assaulted in public spaces

In a nationwide survey, 80% of women reported that they or one of their friends had been drugged while at a bar or club

1 in 2 people in the LGBTQ+ community have themselves or their friends experienced sexual harassment.
