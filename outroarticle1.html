5 COCKTAILS YOU HAVE TO TRY IN NYC

october 3, 2018



So, you’re thirsty. You’ve come to the right place. One of the best features I’ve found from using Outro is that it constantly is introducing me to bars I would have never found otherwise bringing me a totally unique assortment of new experiences.

1. The Sharkeye

MOTHER OF PEARL - EAST VILLAGE, MANHATTAN

95 Avenue A, New York, NY 10009

Not only is this drink absolutely delicious, but the way it’s served is the perfect conversation piece. Normally, I’m not a bourbon fan but who knew it mixed so well with a fresh passion fruit base? This isn’t the typical fall drink but it will absolutely make you reminisce on all the flavors of summer. The drink comes out in a shark shaped glass worthy of its own ooo's and aahh's. The server then sprinkles a ruby-red maraschino cherry glaze on top of the shark to add even more flavor, literally and figuratively. This is the perfect cocktail to order on a first date, giving you plenty of content to talk about for the rest of the night.

INSTAGRAMABILITY: 9


FLAVOR: 9




2. The Pumpkin Head

CANDLE 79 - UPPER EAST SIDE, MANHATTAN

154 E 79th St, New York, NY 10075

If you are familiar with that inner warmth that can only be achieved during late October, when you feel a crisp breeze kindly creep through your window but you don’t care because you’re too cozy under your blanket/turtle neck combo, then you’ll like this drink. It is liquid fall. The pumpkin and cinnamon combo and rock vodka gives this drink a flirty undertone. It’s enough for you to want to curl up next to a fireplace and sip on it all night- or just order a couple more with dinner.

INSTAGRAMABILITY: 9


FLAVOR: 9
